ABOUT ME

Graphic Designer. Brand Strategist. Creator.

I'm Favour Onasanya TL — a visual designer with 5 years of hands-on experience building brands that don't just look good, but perform. I work with startups, personal brands, and businesses across social media, print, packaging, digital, and full brand systems.

I've worked with Justprints Limited, Frootify, Janna's Bowls, Teolyx Digital Solutions, and over 100+ clients globally. I also design high-impact pitch decks and brand presentations for startups and businesses — from Afren AI to Frootify to DeltaDiane.

TL stands for Tree of Life. Everything created under this name is built to grow, breathe, and stand the test of time.

My philosophy? Design without strategy is just decoration. Every pixel is intentional.
